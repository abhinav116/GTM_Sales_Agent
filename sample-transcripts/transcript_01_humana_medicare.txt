Our internal actuaries estimate we are leaving somewhere between 40 and 60 million dollars of RAF capture on the table annually. That number is what prompted this conversation.

Priya Nair: That is helpful. AWS and Epic are both environments RAAPID integrates with natively. On the near real-time side, our pipeline typically processes updated charts within 24 to 48 hours of encounter, which closes that lag almost entirely.
